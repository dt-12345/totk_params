"The Stone Slab at the Floating Ring Ruin:
Some Translation Notes"
　　　　　　　　　　　     -Dictated to Paya{{pageBreak}}"...promise...Zelda...solemnly signed...
Mineru, Sage of...{{pageBreak}}"...hide the key...dragon land...
southeast... Entrust...future...wish...
Defeat...Demon King..."{{pageBreak}}What this tells us is that the five stone
slabs that fell on the village were written
by some sort of sage called Mineru.{{pageBreak}}"Dragon land" and "southeast" are most
likely pointing to the Zonai ruins in Faron.{{pageBreak}}A key has been hidden there, and it's
related to Zelda and the Demon King.
