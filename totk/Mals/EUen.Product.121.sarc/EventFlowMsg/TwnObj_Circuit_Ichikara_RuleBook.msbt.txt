Rules.

---
label: Talk_0002
---
To successfully complete the race, you
must touch {{color id="0"}}all of the lights {{color id="-1"}}and return
to the starting point within the time limit.
{{color id="0"}}Letting go of the device {{color id="-1"}}or {{color id="0"}}going too far
off course{{color id="-1"}} will result in a failure.

You must use the provided steering stick,
but otherwise, you are free to customize
your vehicle as you like.
