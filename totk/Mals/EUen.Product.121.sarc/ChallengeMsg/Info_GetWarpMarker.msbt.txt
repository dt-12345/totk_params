Presenting: The Travel Medallion!

---
label: Step01
---
The Purah Pad has a feature called the
{{color id="0"}}travel medallion{{color id="-1"}}, which allows you to set a
location that you can instantly return to.
But the {{color id="0"}}prototype{{color id="-1"}} you need to activate this
feature was left at the Akkala Ancient Tech Lab.

It's rumored that the lab has been taken over
by intruders...

---
label: Step02
---
The Purah Pad has a feature called the
{{color id="0"}}travel medallion{{color id="-1"}}, which allows you to set a
location that you can instantly return to.
You picked up the prototype needed to activate
this feature from the Akkala Ancient Tech Lab.

Give it to Robbie at the Purah Pad Research
Lab near Hateno Village.

---
label: Step03
---
You can now use the {{color id="0"}}travel medallion{{color id="-1"}} to
instantly return to any location you have set.
The pad can hold two more {{color id="0"}}travel medallions{{color id="-1"}}.

Once {{color id="0"}}10 regions have been mapped{{color id="-1"}}, a second
travel medallion can be activated. The travel
medallion is accessible from your Key Items.

---
label: Step04
---
You can now use two {{color id="0"}}travel medallions{{color id="-1"}} to
instantly return to any location you have set.
The pad can hold one more {{color id="0"}}travel medallion{{color id="-1"}}.

Once {{color id="0"}}15 regions have been mapped{{color id="-1"}}, a third
{{color id="0"}}travel medallion{{color id="-1"}} can be activated. The {{color id="0"}}travel
medallions{{color id="-1"}} are accessible from your Key Items.

---
label: Complete
---
You gave Robbie all the data he needed to
upgrade the Purah Pad once again. You can
now use three {{color id="0"}}travel medallions {{color id="-1"}}to instantly
return to any location you have set. The travel
medallions are accessible from your Key Items.
